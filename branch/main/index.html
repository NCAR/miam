MIAM#

Model-Independent Aerosol Module — a C++ library for representing aerosol and cloud particle populations and their chemical and physical transformations, designed to plug into the MICM chemistry solver.

MIAM is part of the MUSICA family of atmospheric chemistry tools developed at NSF NCAR.
